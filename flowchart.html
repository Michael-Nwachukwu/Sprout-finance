Borrower Journey

THE INVOICE MINTING WIZARD

1
Onboarding
Connect Wallet & Accounting Suite
User connects their MetaMask (Polkadot Hub Hub) and authorizes QuickBooks via OAuth.
2
Selection
Select Unpaid Invoice
The UI displays live unpaid B2B invoices. User selects one and sets their desired financing amount.
3
Evidence
Upload Supporting Documents
User uploads relevant PDFs (Purchase Orders, Bills of Lading). Files are cryptographically pinned to IPFS.
4
Identity
Sign Legal Assignment (World ID)
User clicks "Sign Document". A World ID zero-knowledge proof verifies unique humanhood without exposing KYC data.
5
Action
Submit Mint Request
User clicks "Mint NFT". The transaction is sent on-chain to trigger the autonomous Risk Engines.
6
Funding
Receive USDC Funding
The NFT acts as collateral for the Lending Pool. Once lenders fully fund the pool, USDC is sent to the borrower's wallet.

UNDER THE HOOD: ORACLES

Gemini AI Guardian

Parses invoice JSON + uploaded docs to detect anomalies, flagging discrepancies in terms.

Acurast TEE Engine

A secure off-chain enclave fetches live FX rates and computes Risk Tier and Interest Rate using historical data.

UNDER THE HOOD: EVM

InvoiceNFT.sol

Verifies Oracle secp256k1 signatures and mints an ERC-721 token representing the legal debt.

LendingPool.sol

Handles fractional USDC deposits from lenders and handles the release of funds to the borrower.

✓

1. Timely Repayment

Borrower repays loan in USDC on or before maturity date. Smart contract distributes yield to lenders instantly.

!

2. Grace Period Warning

If maturity date is reached without repayment, the borrower's dashboard turns red, and late notices are sent.

✗

3. Formal Default

If overdue > 7 days, lenders can call triggerDefault(). The Protocol Insurance Pool steps in to cover lender losses.
